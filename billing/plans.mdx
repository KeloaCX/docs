## Features by plan

### Starter — free

- Web chat widget
- 1 AI agent · 50 replies / month
- 2 MB knowledge base
- 1 integration slot (Shopify or custom HTTP)
- Community support

Good for: trying Keloa, single-person businesses, personal projects.

### Growth — €49 / month

Everything in Starter, plus:

- 2 AI agents · 1,500 replies / month
- **Email channel** (up to 3) + chat widget
- 20 MB knowledge base · **Q&A pairs**
- **Flow automations** (up to 3) · CSAT collection
- **Internal notes** with @mentions
- Email support, 1-business-day SLA

Good for: small teams handling daily customer chat.

### Business — €149 / month

Everything in Growth, plus:

- 5 AI agents · 6,000 replies / month
- 100 MB knowledge base · **auto-recrawl** (weekly)
- **Working hours** · **proactive widget triggers**
- **Advanced analytics** · **handoff rules**
- **SLA timers** · **custom inbox views**
- **Unbranded widget**
- Priority support, 4-hour SLA

Good for: growing teams that want serious automation.

### Scale — €449 / month

Everything in Business, plus:

- Unlimited AI agents, channels, and integrations within fair-use
- 25,000 replies / month · 1 GB knowledge base
- Auto-recrawl up to **daily** cadence
- **SSO (SAML / OIDC)** + **audit log export**
- Dedicated CSM, 1-hour SLA
- Custom data residency on request

Good for: mid-market and multi-brand.

## Top up and auto-recharge

If you hit your monthly cap on a paid plan, buy a one-time top-up for **€30 / 1,000 replies**, or enable **auto-recharge** at the same price/pack to top up automatically when you fall below the threshold. See [Usage & credits](/billing/usage-and-credits).

## Choose a plan

Sidebar → **Settings → Plan & billing** → pick a plan → **Upgrade**.

Keloa redirects you to Stripe Checkout. Pay with a card, iDEAL, SEPA direct debit, or Apple/Google Pay. After payment, the new caps apply instantly.

## Changing plans

- **Upgrade** is immediate and prorated.
- **Downgrade** takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Caps reduce then.
- **Cancel** stops renewal; the workspace reverts to Starter caps at period end.

## Yearly billing

Yearly billing saves ~20% and is a single up-front charge. Change from monthly → yearly at any time (prorated).
