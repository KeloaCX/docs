Keloa is an AI-first customer engagement platform built for teams that want one clean inbox for every channel and an AI agent that picks up routine questions around the clock.

## Built for the EU

Keloa is built in the Netherlands and hosted in the EU. Your data stays on the continent, and privacy controls live in the product — export, retention, and contact deletion are all one click in **Settings → Data &amp; privacy**.
