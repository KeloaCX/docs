The **Analytics** dashboard gives you the four or five numbers that actually matter, without burying you in charts.

## Top metrics

| Metric | What it tells you |
| --- | --- |
| **Conversations** | Total opened in the period |
| **AI-resolved** | How many the AI handled end-to-end (no human touch). Your deflection rate. |
| **First response time** | Median time from inbound to first reply (AI or human) |
| **Resolution time** | Median time from open to resolved |
| **CSAT** | % 👍 out of all rated conversations |

## Time range

Top-right: pick a range (today, last 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, custom). All metrics and charts update together.

## Breakdowns

Each chart can be broken down by:

- **Channel** (webchat, email, WhatsApp, …) — see where volume lives
- **Tag** — see which topics drive volume
- **Assignee** — workload per teammate
- **AI agent** — useful if you run several agents

Click a bar/line segment to filter the whole dashboard to that slice.

## Conversations over time

A daily line chart of inbound volume. Spikes usually correspond to outages, launches, or campaign sends — hover the spike to see which tags were unusually high that day.

## AI deflection

A stacked bar:

- **AI resolved** — AI handled it end-to-end
- **AI assisted** — AI replied at least once, human took over to resolve
- **Human only** — no AI involvement

A healthy setup sits around **50–70% AI resolved** with most of the rest AI-assisted. If AI-resolved is low (<20%), your knowledge base probably needs more Q&amp;A pairs — those are the single biggest lever. See [Q&amp;A pairs](/knowledge/qa-pairs).

## Response times

Two views: first-response and resolution. Median and 90th percentile lines, plus a distribution histogram. Break down by channel or teammate to find where the slow queue is.

## CSAT

Overall satisfaction %, trend over time, breakdown by agent and tag. Low CSAT on a specific tag is a clue — e.g. bad ratings on `returns` usually means your return policy is harsher than customers expect.

## Exporting

Every chart has **⋯ → Export CSV** for the raw numbers behind it. Handy for monthly board reports.

## Plan availability

Analytics is available on every plan. The **AI deflection** and **CSAT** charts require AI and CSAT to actually be running — you'll see an empty state telling you how to enable them.
